Lasagna



Back to Index

Description

Here is a recipe for Lasagna:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles, cooked according to package directions
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat. Drain off any excess grease.
  3. Stir in onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes, or until sauce has thickened.
  5. In a large bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. To assemble lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture, and then another layer of meat sauce.
  8. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of lasagna noodles and meat sauce.
  9. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes, or until heated through.
  11. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
