Nasi Goreng


Back to Index

Description

Here is a recipe for Nasi Goreng:

Ingredients

  • 500 grams white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 grams sausage, chopped
  • 100 grams meatballs, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 red chili pepper, sliced diagonally
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chicken powder
  • Green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and onion and cook until softened, about 2 minutes.
  2. Add sausage, meatballs, and carrot and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add eggs and scramble until cooked through, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add rice, sweet soy sauce, salt, pepper, and chicken powder. Stir to combine and cook until rice is heated through, about 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in green onion and serve immediately.
