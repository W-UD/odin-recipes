Korean Fried Chicken Recipe



Back to Index

Description

Here is a recipe for Korean Fried Chicken:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup potato starch
  • 1 tablespoon gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, plus more for frying
  • 1/4 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Steps


  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, cornstarch, potato starch, gochugaru, garlic powder, ginger powder, and black pepper. Toss to coat.

  2. Heat 1/4 cup vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and fry in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 5 minutes per batch.

  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the gochujang, honey, ketchup, soy sauce, and sesame oil.

  6. Add the chicken back to the skillet and toss with the sauce mixture until evenly coated. Garnish with green onions and serve immediately.
